The First "MAT" I did was supposed to resemble ice and snow, as if it was chiseled out of a block of ice and left outside in the winter. 
Back in July 2020 I had the idea to try to paint Substance Painter "MAT" a week for the rest of the year, sadly that plan didn't last long.
